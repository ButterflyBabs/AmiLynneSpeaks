# AmiLynne "Babs" Carroll — Speaker Reel Script
## "The Next Faithful Step"
**Runtime: 2.5-3 minutes**

---

## OPENING (0:00-0:20)

*[Soft, contemplative music begins. Visual: Slow fade from black to Babs in her chair, natural light, warm smile.]*

**BABS:**
"I used to think transformation meant fixing what was broken. Then a stroke left me quadriplegic—and I discovered something else entirely."

*[Pause. Let the weight land.]*

"Transformation isn't about fixing. It's about remembering who you are when everything else falls away."

*[Music swells slightly. Title card: "AmiLynne 'Babs' Carroll"]*

---

## THE INTERRUPtion (0:20-0:50)

*[Visual: Quick montage—hospital imagery (soft, not graphic), early recovery moments, learning to speak again, first time in the wheelchair.]*

**BABS (V.O.):**
"In 2017, my life changed without permission. I lost the use of my body. My voice. My independence. The future I'd planned evaporated in an instant."

*[Visual: Babs now, looking directly at camera.]*

**BABS:**
"I had two choices. I could fight my new reality—or I could learn to work with it. I could demand the old map—or I could discover a new way forward."

*[Pause]*

"I chose the next faithful step."

---

## THE DISCOVERY (0:50-1:25)

*[Visual: Babs speaking at an event, audience listening intently. Cut to: LifeCharter workshop, people writing, engaging.]*

**BABS (V.O.):**
"Through that journey, I discovered something that changed everything: limitation is not the end of command."

*[Visual: Back to Babs direct to camera.]*

**BABS:**
"I rebuilt my life, my voice, my work—one faithful step at a time. I created LifeCharter, a framework for designing a life that actually fits who you are. I started Conversations of Consequence, reaching thousands with a simple message: you don't have to have it all figured out. You just need the next faithful step."

*[Visual: Montage of Babs speaking at different events—faith community, healthcare conference, university, women's gathering.]*

---

## THE MESSAGE (1:25-2:00)

*[Music becomes more driving, purposeful. Visual: Babs on stage, commanding presence.]*

**BABS:**
"Now I speak to audiences who are facing their own interruptions. Healthcare workers drowning in compassion fatigue. Leaders making decisions under pressure. People who've been told their limitations define them."

*[Cut to: Audience members nodding, taking notes, emotional reactions.]*

**BABS (V.O.):**
"I don't offer empty motivation. I offer something more valuable: practical tools for pausing, assessing, and deciding from alignment instead of fear."

*[Visual: Babs back to camera, intimate.]*

**BABS:**
"I teach the Yellow Light—the sacred pause between stimulus and response where wisdom lives. I help people remember that fear is just misunderstood imagination. I show them how to navigate trauma without becoming identified by it."

*[Pause, let it breathe.]*

"Most importantly? I remind them who they are—and that the next faithful step is always within reach."

---

## THE INVITATION (2:00-2:30)

*[Music lifts, hopeful. Visual: Babs speaking to large audience, then intimate small group, then back to direct camera.]*

**BABS:**
"If your audience is facing change they didn't choose... if they're tired of performative resilience and ready for practical alignment... if they need permission to pause and remember who they are..."

*[Direct to camera, warm smile.]*

"I'd love to speak with them."

*[Title card: "Book AmiLynne to Speak"]*

**BABS (V.O.):**
"Head up, wings out."

*[Visual: Babs in her chair, butterfly accent, warm smile. Fade to gold.]*

*[End card: amilynne-speaks.vercel.app | amilynne@amilynnecarroll.com]*

- Warm, natural lighting
- Mix of intimate close-ups and confident stage presence

- Butterfly motif subtly integrated

- Opening: Contemplative, piano or acoustic guitar
- Middle: Builds with strings and light percussion
- End: Uplifting, hopeful, resolves to quiet confidence
