Why AmiLynne Speaks

AmiLynne speaks because she knows what it is to have life interrupted, identity shaken, and the body become unfamiliar territory.

After a stroke left her quadriplegic, AmiLynne rebuilt her life, voice, work, and calling through faith, adaptability, and the sacred practice of taking one faithful step at a time. Her message is not about pretending hardship is easy. It is about discovering that limitation is not the end of command.

"I don't speak to tell people how to fix their lives. I speak to remind them who they are—and that the next faithful step is always within reach."

— AmiLynne "Babs" Carroll

Signature Keynotes

AmiLynne's keynotes are designed to meet audiences in the holy middle: the space between what happened and what becomes possible next. Each message blends lived experience, spiritual depth, trauma-informed wisdom, and practical next steps.

⚡

Power of Adaptability

Reclaim Command When Life Changes Without Permission

Best for: Healthcare, disability inclusion, leadership, faith-based events, universities, resilience conferences

When a stroke left AmiLynne "Babs" Carroll quadriplegic, life did not simply change. It demanded a new relationship with body, voice, identity, faith, and purpose. In this powerful keynote, AmiLynne shows audiences that adaptability is not passive acceptance. It is sacred command in motion.

She helps audiences understand how to reclaim agency when circumstances cannot be controlled, how to respond rather than collapse, and how to take the next faithful step when the old map no longer works.

Audience Takeaways

  • How to reclaim command in uncontrollable circumstances
  • Why adaptability is spiritual strength, not surrender
  • How to move from fear-driven reaction to aligned response
  • A practical framework for pausing, assessing, and deciding
🛑

Yellow Light Leadership

Pause. Assess. Decide.

Best for: Corporate leadership, nonprofit teams, ministry leaders, educators, entrepreneurs, caregivers

Most people live between red-light panic and green-light performance. But transformation often begins in the yellow light: the sacred pause where wisdom returns.

In this keynote, AmiLynne teaches the Yellow Light practice as a leadership, communication, and resilience tool. Audiences learn how to slow reaction, listen beneath pressure, and take the next faithful step with clarity.

Audience Takeaways

  • How to interrupt reactive decision-making
  • How to use the pause as a leadership tool
  • How to communicate from alignment instead of urgency
  • How to build steadier teams, families, and communities
💫

Fear Is Misunderstood Imagination

Reclaiming the Creative Power Beneath Anxiety

Best for: Personal growth events, colleges, women's conferences, faith communities, mental wellness events

What if fear is not the enemy? What if fear is imagination that has forgotten its holy assignment?

AmiLynne reframes fear as creative energy that has been pointed toward threat instead of possibility. Through story, spiritual insight, and practical tools, she helps audiences transform fear from a prison into a doorway.

Audience Takeaways

  • A fresh, compassionate way to understand fear
  • How to redirect anxious imagination toward vision
  • How to calm the nervous system without shaming the soul
  • How to take action when fear is present but no longer in charge
🧭

From Trauma to True North

Designing a Life After the Life You Expected

Best for: Trauma-informed events, recovery communities, coaching conferences, healthcare, caregiver events, spiritual growth gatherings

Trauma can fracture identity, blur purpose, and make the future feel like foreign land. But trauma does not get the final authorship.

In this keynote, AmiLynne guides audiences through the process of returning to their True North: values, identity, vision, purpose, and strategy. This is not a speech about "getting over it." It is about learning to live truthfully after what happened.

Audience Takeaways

  • How to name what happened without becoming what happened
  • How to reconnect with values and purpose
  • How to begin designing life after disruption
  • How to move from survival identity to sacred authorship
👂

Conversations of Consequence

Hearing the Still Small Voice in a Loud World

Best for: Faith communities, retreats, churches, spiritual growth conferences, leadership events

The world is loud. Fear is loud. Pressure is loud. But the voice that changes everything is often still, small, and easily overlooked.

AmiLynne helps audiences recognize the difference between noise, fear, ego, urgency, and divine guidance. This keynote invites people into deeper listening, courageous honesty, and conversations that actually change a life.

Audience Takeaways

  • How to listen beneath fear and pressure
  • How to recognize sacred guidance in ordinary moments
  • How to have healing conversations without abandoning truth
  • How to build a life guided by divine alignment
🔮

Sacred Alchemy

Bridging Brain, Body, and Soul on the Path to Wholeness

Best for: Wellness events, healthcare, trauma recovery, spiritual conferences, mind-body-spirit gatherings

Wholeness is not the absence of limitation. Wholeness is the remembering of truth across brain, body, and soul.

In this keynote, AmiLynne explores the sacred relationship between thought, body, faith, identity, and healing. She invites audiences to stop treating the body as a battlefield and begin seeing it as part of the conversation.

Audience Takeaways

  • How to relate to the body with compassion and wisdom
  • How trauma, thought, and identity shape lived experience
  • How spiritual truth can support practical healing
  • How to pursue wholeness without demanding perfection
