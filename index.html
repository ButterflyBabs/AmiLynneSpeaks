Why AmiLynne Speaks

AmiLynne speaks because she knows what it is to have life interrupted, identity shaken, and the body become unfamiliar territory.

After a stroke left her quadriplegic, AmiLynne rebuilt her life, voice, work, and calling through faith, adaptability, and the sacred practice of taking one faithful step at a time. Her message is not about pretending hardship is easy. It is about discovering that limitation is not the end of command.

"I don't speak to tell people how to fix their lives. I speak to remind them who they are—and that the next faithful step is always within reach."

— AmiLynne "Babs" Carroll

Signature Keynotes

Transformative talks that meet your audience where they are and guide them toward alignment

The LifeCharter: Designing a Life of Alignment

Introduce your audience to the 12-dimension framework for intentional living. From career to relationships, spirituality to finances—discover how to build a life that reflects your true values, not someone else's expectations.

Ideal for: Leadership, Personal Development, Corporate

The Yellow Light: From Reaction to Response

Learn the transformative practice of the Yellow Yield—creating sacred pause between stimulus and response. Perfect for audiences dealing with overwhelm, anxiety, or the constant pressure to react.

Ideal for: Healthcare, Education, High-Stress Professions

Remembering Who You Are

Transformation isn't about fixing what's broken—it's about remembering what's true. This talk guides audiences through the journey from survival patterns to sacred identity, from fear to faith.

Ideal for: Spiritual, Recovery, Trauma-Informed Events

Adaptability as Spiritual Practice

For faith-based and spiritual audiences: discover how limitation becomes the doorway to deeper trust, and how adaptability is not surrender but sacred responsiveness to life's unfolding.

Ideal for: Churches, Retreats, Faith-Based Organizations

The Next Faithful Step

When the path ahead is unclear and the mountain seems too high, the only thing required is the next faithful step. A practical, encouraging message for anyone facing uncertainty or transition.

Command from the Chair: Disability as Teacher

An honest, tender, and empowering message about living with disability—whether visible or invisible. For accessibility events, healthcare professionals, and anyone seeking to understand resilience from the inside out.

Ideal for: Disability Events, Healthcare, Accessibility Advocacy
