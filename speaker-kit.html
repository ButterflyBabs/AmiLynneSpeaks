About AmiLynne

AmiLynne "Babs" Carroll helps audiences move from fear to faith, from survival to alignment, and from overwhelm to purposeful action. Through story, spiritual insight, and practical tools, she guides people to reclaim clarity, courage, and command when life changes without permission.

After a stroke left her quadriplegic, AmiLynne rebuilt her life, voice, work, and calling through faith, adaptability, and the sacred practice of taking one faithful step at a time. She is the creator of LifeCharter, host of Conversations of Consequence DAILY, and a sought-after speaker on adaptability, resilience, trauma-informed transformation, and practical alignment.

Signature Keynotes

  • Power of Adaptability
    Reclaim Command When Life Changes Without Permission
    When a stroke left AmiLynne quadriplegic, life demanded a new relationship with body, voice, identity, faith, and purpose. This keynote shows audiences that adaptability is sacred command in motion—not passive acceptance.
    Healthcare • Disability • Leadership • Faith • Universities
  • Yellow Light Leadership
    Pause. Assess. Decide.
    Most people live between red-light panic and green-light performance. But transformation begins in the yellow light: the sacred pause where wisdom returns. Learn to slow reaction, listen beneath pressure, and decide from alignment.
    Corporate • Nonprofit • Ministry • Education • Entrepreneurs
  • Fear Is Misunderstood Imagination
    Reclaiming the Creative Power Beneath Anxiety
    What if fear is imagination that forgot its holy assignment? AmiLynne reframes fear as creative energy pointed toward threat instead of possibility—transforming fear from prison into doorway.
    Personal Growth • Colleges • Women's Events • Faith • Wellness
  • From Trauma to True North
    Designing a Life After the Life You Expected
    Trauma can fracture identity and blur purpose, but it doesn't get final authorship. This keynote guides audiences through returning to True North: values, identity, vision, purpose, and strategy.
    Trauma-Informed • Recovery • Healthcare • Caregivers • Spiritual Growth
  • Conversations of Consequence
    Hearing the Still Small Voice in a Loud World
    The world is loud. Fear is loud. Pressure is loud. But the voice that changes everything is often still, small, and easily overlooked. Learn to recognize divine guidance in ordinary moments.
    Faith Communities • Retreats • Churches • Spiritual Conferences
  • Sacred Alchemy
    Bridging Brain, Body, and Soul on the Path to Wholeness
    Wholeness is not the absence of limitation. It is the remembering of truth across brain, body, and soul. Stop treating the body as a battlefield and begin seeing it as part of the conversation.
    Wellness • Healthcare • Trauma Recovery • Mind-Body-Spirit
